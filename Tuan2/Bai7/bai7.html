A smile is a facial expression formed primarily by flexing the muscles at the sides of the mouth. It is believed it takes about 80 muscles to smile. Some smiles include a constraction of the muscles at the corner of the eyes, an action known as a "Duchenne smile". Smiles performed without the eye constraction may be perceived ad insinsere.
